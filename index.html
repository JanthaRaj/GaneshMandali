About Janatha Raj

A community united in devotion and celebration

Janatha Raj is a vibrant community group dedicated to celebrating Ganesh Chaturthi with traditional fervor and modern enthusiasm. For over 15 years, we have been bringing together families and friends to honor Lord Ganesha through our annual celebration.
